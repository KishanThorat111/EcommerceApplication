Enable Third-Party Cookies

Our application requires third-party cookies to function correctly. Please enable them in your browser settings.

Please follow these steps to enable third-party cookies:

  • Google Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data. Enable "Allow all cookies."
  • Firefox: Go to Options > Privacy & Security. Under "Cookies and Site Data," select "Accept cookies and site data from websites."
  • Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy. Uncheck "Block all cookies."
  • Microsoft Edge: Go to Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data. Enable "Allow sites to save and read cookie data."
